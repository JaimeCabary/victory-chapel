About Our Pastor


Pastor Emeka Victor Samuel

Pastor Emeka Victor Samuel is the visionary and shepherd of Victory Life Global Outreach Ministry (Victory Chapel Owerri). A devoted servant of God, he has dedicated his life to spreading the message of Christ, nurturing disciples, and building a loving, Christ-centered community.

Under his faithful leadership, Victory Chapel has become a beacon of hope — a place where every person is embraced, inspired, and empowered to live a victorious life. Pastor Emeka is passionate about preaching the undiluted Word of God and seeing lives transformed by the Holy Spirit. His heart shines forth in every service, counseling session, and pastoral visit he conducts.



Pastor Emeka Victor Samuel and His family

With his loving wife,three beautiful daughters and son, Pastor Emeka Victor Samuel leads the church with grace, humility, and an unfailing commitment to serving God’s people. Together, they guide families, nurture spiritual growth, and build a strong foundation for generations to come at Victory Chapel Owerri.
