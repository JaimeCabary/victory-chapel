Our Ministries

At Victory Chapel, we have a variety of ministries dedicated to nurturing spiritual growth, building strong families, and serving the community with love and grace.

Children’s Ministry

Raising the next generation with a foundation rooted in Christ’s love and teaching.

Women’s Ministry

Empowering and supporting women through prayer, teaching, and fellowship.

Men’s Ministry

Building strong spiritual leaders and role models within families and the community.

Youth Ministry

Guiding our youth to find their purpose and stand firm in their walk with Christ.
